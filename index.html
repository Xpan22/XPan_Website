Plant Community & Ecosystem Ecology

I investigate how plant diversity, functional traits, and global-change drivers shape the functioning and temporal stability of grassland ecosystems.

My research combines large-scale grassland experiments, global ecological networks, and trait-based approaches to understand biodiversity-ecosystem functioning relationships.
